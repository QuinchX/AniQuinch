Services

Our customer service is available at all times, and our team responds within 24 hours. This site is free to use, with just a few premium capabilities available, such as free shipping cost, but the main functions that customers use, such as sorting products by price, reviews, and buck-order, are available for free. During the holidays, we regularly offer several events that are open to the public. These events include product freebies, special edition goods, and the possibility to win cash for every purchase made during the event.

Premium Account

AniQuinch provides a premium account service that allows users access to features such as no delivery cost when purchasing products. A premium account cost user for 10$ per month but in return, gives user access to customizable profile, lesser ads and much more. Premium users also get the first access to new items and a reserved spot in limited edition merchandise.

Holiday Events

AniQuinch hosts a limited-time event for each holiday, giving users the opportunity to obtain special merchandise for that occasion. Every user, whether premium or not, has access to the event and additional incentives. There is also an event for every anniversary of the website.

Discount and Sales

AniQuinch is your budget-friendly shopping site; at the beginning and end of each month, all products on the site will be discounted. Discounts from events are stacked to the beginning and end of the month sales, so if a holiday falls at the beginning or end of the month, there will be a significant discount for those goods.
